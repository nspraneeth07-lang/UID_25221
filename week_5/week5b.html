VIRAT KOHLI

Test ODI T20I 

 Virat Kohli (born November 5, 1988) is a legendary Indian international cricketer widely regarded as one of the best all-format batsmen in history.
   A top-order right-handed batter and former captain, he is renowned for his "chase-master" ability, immense fitness, and aggressive style.
    He led India to numerous victories and holds multiple international scoring records.
