LORD VENKATESHWARA

Lord Venkateswara, also known as Balaji, Srinivasa, or Venkatachalapati, is a revered form of Lord Vishnu worshipped primarily at the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Andhra Pradesh, India.

LORD SHIVA

Lord Shiva, also known as Mahadev ("Great God"), is one of the most prominent deities in Hinduism

LORD BRAMHA

Lord Brahma is the Creator god in the Hindu trinity, the Trimurti
